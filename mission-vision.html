Our Mission

To provide a sanctuary for resilience, ritual, and creative truth..

Our Vision

A world where neurodivergent individuals and their caregivers have the tangible, structured support systems they need to navigate the world with confidence and emotional honesty.

Why This Matters:

I am a single mom to three incredible, neurodivergent boys. I know firsthand the exhaustion, the complexity, and the profound, quiet beauty of this journey. Moonlit Journals was born out of my own necessity—a need to regulate, to record, and to create a dedicated space for our family’s "wins" and "struggles."

I created this for us. I created this for the parents, the caregivers, the supporters, the loved ones, those who are carrying the world on their shoulders while supporting their loved ones possibly even through their own unique neurodivergent paths. You are not just a caregiver; you are an anchor, and you deserve a system that holds you, too. The background imagery was captured by my son —a testament to the beauty found in our shared night-time vigils. Moonlit Journals is a living archive of our collective truth, and I’m building it so that no one —especially not the solo caregivers —has to feel like they are navigating this path alone.
